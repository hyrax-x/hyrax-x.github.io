Description

This landing page helps new designers understand why design principles matter. It is based on "24 Principles for Massive Change Your Life and Work" by Bruce Mau, especially "The Ten Most Important Dimensions of Beauty in Design."

Figma Prototype

Full screen for the best experiences.

Research

Me and my group research deeply about design principle and gather examples.

Process

We all contribute to designing every part of the landing page. Because of time constraint, we cannot include all design principles, so we decided to only include the important one.

Final product

This landing page welcomes beginner designers eager to dive deeper into the art of creating great, timeless, and standout designs.
