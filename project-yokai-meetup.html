Description

This is a semester's work for the Visual Narration class. The task is to imagine a Yokai creature within your own folk culture, taking the Japanese Yokai as inspiration meets up with other Yokai.

Sources

  • Shiro-Uneri: yokai.com
  • Vietnamese Dragon: wikipedia.org

Character Design

Like Shiro Uneri's origin, Wet Draguun originated from cleaning a water bucket. It has some visual features of the Vietnamese dragon and Uneri, and sprinkles on top some of my own ideas. Since it came from water, Wet Draguun can use its water ability and fly, even though no one knows whether the water it creates is clean or not. Its personality is quite chaotic, happy, and unhinged, the opposite of Shiro Uneri.
